My Services

Assessments That Make a Difference

Every assessment includes a detailed written report with practical, evidence-based recommendations.

Full Educational Assessment

The most comprehensive way to understand your child's learning profile. Examines cognitive ability, reading, writing, spelling, and maths alongside social and emotional factors.

EHCP Assessments & Reports

Educational psychology evidence for Education, Health and Care Plan applications and annual reviews. Clear, robust reports that local authorities take seriously.

DSA Assessments

Disabled Students' Allowance needs assessments for university students with specific learning difficulties, helping you access the support you're entitled to.

Access Arrangements

Assessment for extra time and other exam accommodations (GCSE, A-Level). Reports meet JCQ requirements so schools can apply with confidence.

Support for Schools

Understanding students' abilities and learning profiles, behaviour management strategies, and training for teaching staff.
